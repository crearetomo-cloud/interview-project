## Core Questions

### Opening Questions (5-7 minutes)

1. **Background/Context**
   - "To start, can you tell me a bit about yourself and what you're working on these days?"
   - Follow-up: "What led you to [current role/project]?"

2. **Current Focus**
   - "What are you most excited about in your work right now?"
   - Follow-up: "What makes that particularly interesting to you?"

### Main Questions (20-30 minutes)

3. **Topic 1: [Topic Name]**
   - "[Main question about topic]"
   - Follow-up: "[Deeper question]"
   - Follow-up: "Can you share a story that illustrates that?"

4. **Topic 2: [Topic Name]**
   - "[Main question about topic]"
   - Follow-up: "[Deeper question]"
   - Follow-up: "What surprised you about [aspect]?"

5. **Topic 3: [Topic Name]**
   - "[Main question about topic]"
   - Follow-up: "[Deeper question]"
   - Follow-up: "How did that change your approach?"

6. **Topic 4: [Topic Name]**
   - "[Main question about topic]"
   - Follow-up: "[Deeper question]"
   - Follow-up: "What would you do differently?"

7. **Topic 5: [Topic Name]**
   - "[Main question about topic]"
   - Follow-up: "[Deeper question]"
   - Follow-up: "What's your take on [related topic]?"

### Deep Dive Questions (10-15 minutes)

8. **Challenges**
   - "What's been the biggest challenge you've faced in [context]?"
   - Follow-up: "How did you overcome that?"
   - Follow-up: "What did you learn from that experience?"

9. **Insights**
   - "What's something most people don't understand about [topic]?"
   - Follow-up: "Why do you think that is?"

10. **Advice**
    - "What advice would you give to someone starting out in [field]?"
    - Follow-up: "What's a common mistake you see people make?"

### Closing Questions (5 minutes)

11. **Future**
    - "What's next for you?"
    - Follow-up: "What are you most looking forward to?"

12. **Final Thoughts**
    - "Is there anything else you'd like to share that we haven't covered?"
    - "Any final thoughts or insights?"

## Closing (2-3 minutes)

**Script:**
"Thank you so much, [Name]. This has been really insightful, and I really appreciate you sharing your story.

Just to recap next steps:
- I'll work on the article over the next [timeframe]
- I'll send you a draft for review before publication
- Once it's published, I'll send you the link
- Feel free to share it with your network if you'd like

Do you have any questions for me?

Thanks again, and I'll be in touch soon!"

**Key Points to Remember:**
