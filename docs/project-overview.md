# Project Overview

## Purpose

This project standardizes the interview process to ensure high-quality, consistent output while maintaining efficiency and professionalism throughout the entire workflow.

## Key Objectives

1. **Consistency**: Standardized templates and processes across all interviews
2. **Quality**: Best practices for conducting and documenting interviews
3. **Efficiency**: Clear workflows and checklists to streamline operations
4. **Professionalism**: Polished outreach, communication, and deliverables

## Project Components

### Documentation
- Process workflows and checklists
- Outreach templates and scripts
- Consent and legal documentation

### Playbooks
- Interview facilitation techniques
- Question design strategies
- Writing and editing guidelines

### Content Templates
- Interview profiles
- Script templates
- Article drafts
- Publishing copy

## Target Audience

This system is designed for:
- Content creators conducting interviews
- Journalists and writers
- Marketing teams
- Researchers

## Success Metrics

- Consistent quality across interviews
- Reduced time from outreach to publication
- High participant satisfaction
- Professional presentation
