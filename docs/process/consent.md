# Interview Consent Form

## Purpose
To obtain clear consent from interviewees regarding recording, usage, and publication of the interview.

## Consent Form Template

**Interview Consent and Release Form**

**Interviewee Name:** [Name]  
**Interviewer Name:** [Your Name]  
**Date:** [Date]  
**Project:** [Project Name]

### Recording Consent

- [ ] I consent to this interview being recorded (audio/video)
- [ ] I understand the recording will be used for transcription and content creation
- [ ] I understand the recording may be archived for reference

### Usage Rights

- [ ] I grant permission for my words, quotes, and insights to be used in written articles
- [ ] I understand the content will be published on [platform/publication]
- [ ] I grant permission for the content to be shared on social media and other channels
- [ ] I understand the content may be used for promotional purposes

### Review Rights

- [ ] I understand I will have the opportunity to review the article before publication
- [ ] I understand I can request changes to quotes and factual information
- [ ] I understand I cannot request changes to the overall narrative or structure (unless factually incorrect)

### Attribution

- [ ] I consent to being identified by name in the published article
- [ ] I consent to my title/role being included
- [ ] I consent to my company/organization being mentioned
- [ ] I consent to my photo being used (if applicable)

### Additional Terms

- [ ] I understand I will receive a copy of the final published article
- [ ] I understand I may be asked to share the article (but am not required to)
- [ ] I understand this consent is voluntary and can be withdrawn before publication

## Digital Consent (For Remote Interviews)

If conducting remote interviews, you can adapt this to a digital format:

1. Send consent form via email before the interview
2. Review key points at the start of the interview
3. Get verbal confirmation on recording
4. Follow up with signed form after interview

## Best Practices

- Always obtain consent before recording
- Be transparent about how the content will be used
- Honor review requests and corrections
- Respect boundaries if someone declines certain uses
- Keep signed consent forms on file
- Be clear about what can and cannot be changed during review

## Legal Considerations

- Consult with legal counsel for commercial publications
- Ensure compliance with local recording laws
- Consider GDPR/privacy regulations if applicable
- Maintain records of all consent forms
