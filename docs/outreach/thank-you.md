# Thank You Message Template

## Purpose
Express gratitude after the interview and maintain the relationship.

## Immediate Follow-up (Within 24 hours)

Hi [Name],

Thank you so much for taking the time to speak with me today! I really enjoyed our conversation about [key topic discussed]. Your insights on [specific point] were particularly valuable.

I'll be working on the article over the next [timeframe] and will share a draft with you for review before publication. I'll also send you the final piece once it's live.

Thanks again for sharing your story!

Best,
[Your Name]

## After Publication

Hi [Name],

The article is now live! You can find it here: [link]

Thank you again for your time and for sharing your insights. I really appreciate it, and I think the piece turned out great.

Feel free to share it with your network if you'd like!

Best,
[Your Name]

## Tips

- Send thank you within 24 hours of the interview
- Reference something specific from the conversation
- Set expectations about next steps
- Follow through on promises (draft review, final link, etc.)
- Keep the relationship warm for potential future collaboration
