# Share Request Template

## Purpose
Politely request the interviewee to share the published article.

## Template

Hi [Name],

Hope you're doing well! The article we worked on together is now live: [link]

I'd be thrilled if you'd be willing to share it with your network. I think your audience would really appreciate your insights on [topic].

Here's a suggested post you could use (feel free to modify):

---

[Suggested social media post text]

---

No pressure at all - just wanted to make it easy if you'd like to share!

Thanks again for everything,
[Your Name]

## Tips

- Make it easy - provide a ready-to-use post
- Give them the option to customize
- Be gracious if they don't share
- Don't be pushy - one request is enough
- Thank them regardless of whether they share

## Suggested Post Templates

### LinkedIn
"Excited to share my conversation with [Your Name] about [topic]. We discussed [key point 1] and [key point 2]. Check it out: [link]"

### Twitter/X
"Just published my interview with [Name] about [topic]. Key insights: [brief point]. Read more: [link]"

### Instagram
"Had a great conversation with [Name] about [topic]. Swipe to see some highlights, and check the link in bio for the full interview! [link]"
